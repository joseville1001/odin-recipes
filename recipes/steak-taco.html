Steak Taco

Description

A steak, cheese, and refried bean taco in a spinach tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Tip Steak Thin
  • Seasonings
    • Garlic and Salt powder
    • Adobo Goya
    • Cumin
    • Salt
  • Oil
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper
  • 3 Spinach Tortillas
  • 3 Mozarella Cheese sticks
  • 1 Refried Beans can

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in a pan on medium low heat.
  2. While oil is heating, wash, cut, and season steak.
  3. Once oil is hot, place steak on pan, and sear on both sides.
  4. While steak is searing, dice tomatoes, onion, and bell pepper.
  5. Once steak is seared, add tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers and let them sear.
  6. On another pan, heat and brown 3 tortillas. Also, heat and melt the cheese sticks on top of the tortillas.
  7. Once veggies are seared, open can of refried beans and heat it up in the same pan.
  8. Once tortillas and refried beans are hot, serve the tacos -- tortilla, cheese, steak, veggies, beans, wrap.
