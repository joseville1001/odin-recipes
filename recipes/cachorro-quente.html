Cachorro Quente

Description

Add flavor and savor to your hot dog like the Brazilians with a tomato, onion, and bell pepper guiso.

Ingredients

  • Beef Franks
  • Seasame Hoagie rolls
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper
  • Adobo Goya

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in a medium pot on medium low heat.
  2. While oil is heating, dice tomatoes, onion, and bell pepper.
  3. Once oil is hot, place diced veggies in pot, set to medium heat, and let sear.
  4. While veggies are searing, cut each frank in 3 using 2 diagonal cuts.
  5. Once veggies are seared, add Adobo Goya, and stir. Also, add the franks and stir.
  6. Seat heat to medium low and cover. Wait 5-10 minutes.
  7. Serve - hoagie, then franks, and guiso. Enjoy with condiments -- ketchup, mustard, mayo.
